# Notes for the video "**Flexbox Alignment and Centering with justify-content**"

## Fundamental Terms Learned
- `justify-content`

## Code Learned
- `justify-content: flex-start || flex-end || center || space-between || space-around`

## Tips
- `justify-content: flex-start` is the default
- the `justify-content` property is placed in the flex container
- `justify-content` works with `flex-direction: column` property when a height is specified for the flex container
